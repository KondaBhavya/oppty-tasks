Where Ragas Meet Rock

About Agam



Agam is a Bangalore-based music band known for blending traditional Indian Carnatic music with modern rock.Formed in the early 2000s, the band creates a unique sound by combining classical ragas with instruments like electric guitar, drums, and keyboards. Led by vocalist Harish Sivaramakrishnan,Agam has gained popularity for its energetic performances and innovative fusion style, making it one of India’s leading contemporary music bands.



Agam is known for bringing a fresh approach to Indian music by mixing traditional melodies with modern sounds. The band focuses on preserving the beauty of classical music while making it more accessible to today’s audience through instruments like electric guitar and drums. Their songs are rich in musical detail and reflect both cultural depth and contemporary style, which makes them stand out in the Indian music scene.



Agam has become one of India’s leading fusion bands by successfully bridging the gap between classical tradition and modern music. Their compositions often draw from ancient Carnatic kritis and present them with a contemporary twist using instruments like electric guitar, violin, and drums. What sets Agam apart is their ability to maintain the purity of classical music while making it engaging for modern listeners. With a growing fan base and performances at major events, the band continues to inspire a new generation of music lovers through its innovative and soulful sound.

Live Concerts



Agam's live performances are known for their rich musical experience and strong stage presence. Each concert is carefully crafted to take the audience on a journey through classical ragas and modern rock sounds. The band members interact with the audience, creating an engaging and immersive atmosphere that keeps listeners connected throughout the show.



One of the highlights of Agam's concerts is the seamless coordination between instruments, especially the electric guitar, violin, and percussion. The guitar adds a powerful modern edge to the traditional compositions, making the performance both dynamic and energetic. Their concerts often include improvisations, which make every show unique and special.



Agam has performed at various prestigious venues, college festivals, and cultural events across India. Their ability to present classical music in a contemporary format has helped them reach a wider audience. The combination of visual stage presence, lighting, and high-quality sound makes their concerts memorable and impactful.
